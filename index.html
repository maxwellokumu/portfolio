About Victor

I am Victor Omuyanza, a passionate and dedicated Mechatronic Engineering student at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). In this leadership role, I am committed to advocating for student academic welfare, fostering a conducive learning environment, and bridging the gap between students and the university administration. I believe in leveraging both technical expertise and strong leadership to drive positive change and empower my peers.
